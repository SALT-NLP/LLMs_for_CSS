You will read a toxic post from the internet and tell us whether the provided explanation accurately explains why it's toxic.

Explanations come from other annotators. They were told to do the following: "Explain who is targeted by the offensive post (individual or group), which minority or demographic group(s), and what aspects of the group(s) are referenced. Use (overly) simplistic "GROUP is/does/can't ___" phrasing (e.g. "women can't drive", "gay men are feminine", "asians are good at math").

"

When talking about stereoypes, think about the ones that are (unfortunately) culturally present in our society. Perspectives are given from an American cultural frame.

Note: we will assume that MTurk workers only have good intentions when annotating these posts.
